Artist Statement

Mahshad is a singer, songwriter, composer, arranger, performer, and dancer based in Boston. Her music is a unique fusion of musical theater and jazzy pop influences, characterized by deep, meaningful lyrics that explore mental health awareness and themes of personal transformation. Through her artistry, she aims to create emotional connections with her audience, leaving an impact that resonates long after the curtain falls. She is currently enrolled at Berklee College of Music, majoring in Professional Music with tracks in Jazz Composition and Scoring for Media, while also minoring in Musical Theater Performance. Mahshad strives to create performances that bring her artistic visions to life, collaborating with choreographers, costume designers, and other creatives to craft immersive worlds. Through concept albums and theatrical productions, she invites her audience to watch and actively participate, making it a deeply engaging and personal experience. In addition to her live performances, Mahshad is passionate about composing for film and TV, driven by her love for sound design and its power to tell stories. Looking ahead, she aims to make her mark in the growing industries of concept albums, theater, and sound design. By uniting the narrative-driven art with creative approaches, Mahshad believes in the power of storytelling to connect people - a unifying force that underpins her work and bonds her growing fandom.
