Watch My Shows

Looking Back was a powerful, introspective theater piece written by Perri Webb and brought to life by SuperHumanArts, Berklee’s gender-marginalized theater club. I was honored to be part of this production, which explored the themes of time, memory, and self-perception—inviting both performers and audience to reflect on who we’ve been, who we are, and who we’re becoming.
